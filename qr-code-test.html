How to Test in Your Email Client

  1. Go to the main app and navigate to Dashboard
  2. Click on "Preview Email with QR Codes"
  3. Use the "Copy HTML for Email Client" button
  4. Paste the copied HTML into your email client's HTML editor
  5. Send a test email to yourself

The QR codes should appear and be scannable in most modern email clients that support HTML content.
